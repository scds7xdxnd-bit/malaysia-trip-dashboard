Method & caveats

Criteria weights · pairwise AHP · CR = 0.016 (consistent)

Coupled-budget rule. The two AHP winners (Mandarin Oriental + The Edison) overshoot ~RM700 at 4/3 nights. Feasible flagship pairing: Traders KL (AHP #3, best Petronas view) + The Edison = ~RM5,730. Or keep MO and drop to 3 KL nights.

City vs beach. Location weight (0.14) favours Georgetown boutiques. Want a beach half? Mentally zero Location → Rasa Sayang leads on Luxury + Amenities.

Weather & packing. July in KL & Penang: hot (28–33°C), humid (80%+), afternoon thunderstorms. Pack: lightweight cotton/linen, rain jacket, umbrella, sunscreen, insect repellent, comfortable walking shoes, universal adapter (Type G).
